## Wildcard Copies

This trips me up every time I attempt to copy files from a RaspberryPi to my MacBook.

When using a `zsh` shell, you must escape wildcard characters.

## Good

<TerminalBlock language="bash" title="correct — escaped wildcard">
scp pi@123.45.67.89:/home/some/dir/\\* ./data/
</TerminalBlock>

## Bad

<TerminalBlock language="bash" title="incorrect — unescaped wildcard">
scp pi@123.45.67.89:/home/some/dir/* ./data/
</TerminalBlock>

<TIL>In `zsh`, you must escape wildcard characters in `scp` commands with a backslash — `scp host:/path/\*.txt .` — or the shell tries to glob locally.</TIL>
